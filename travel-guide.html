Kumily Travel Guide

About Kumily and Thekkady

Kumily is located in the Idukki district of Kerala and is the gateway to Thekkady and Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.

Top Attractions

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, spice plantation visit, Chellarkovil viewpoint, elephant activities, and boat safari.

Local Experiences

Guests can enjoy Kerala cooking classes, traditional meals, local spice tours, and family hospitality.
