Things To Do in Kumily and Thekkady

Kumily and Thekkady offer beautiful natural attractions, wildlife experiences, spice tours, shopping, and traditional Kerala cooking experiences.

Top local experiences

  • Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Explore spice plantations
  • Take a boat safari in Periyar Lake
  • Enjoy a Kerala cooking class
  • Shop Kerala spices

Why join a Kerala cooking class?

Cooking classes allow visitors to experience Kerala culture, learn spices, and enjoy authentic homemade food. It is one of the most memorable activities for travelers staying in Kumily and Thekkady.
