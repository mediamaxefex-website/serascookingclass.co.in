About Sera's Cooking Class

Welcome to Sera's Cooking Class, located in Kumily near Thekkady. We provide authentic Kerala home cooking experiences for tourists from all over the world.

Our Story

Guests learn real Kerala dishes using fresh spices, coconut, vegetables, and traditional cooking methods. Our classes are friendly, interactive, and suitable for beginners who want a meaningful cultural experience while traveling in Kerala.

You will cook, learn, and enjoy delicious Kerala meals together in a relaxed family environment that feels personal and welcoming.

What makes us special

  • Warm home atmosphere for international visitors
  • Real recipes prepared in a practical way
  • Simple explanation of local ingredients and spices
  • Hands-on class, tasting, and shared dining

Our Kitchen Experience

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a real Kerala cooking experience for tourists visiting Kumily and Thekkady. We believe food connects people, culture, traditions, and travel memories in a powerful way.
